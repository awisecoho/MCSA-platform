# MCSA Course Content — SME Review Notes

## What needs correction across all courses:
1. Remove academic/theoretical framing — this is a field practitioner curriculum
2. Assessment modules should be "Knowledge Check" not just "quiz" — they test application
3. Every module needs a "Field Application" section — what the adjuster does on Monday morning
4. Remove redundant cross-references between courses — each module stands alone
5. NFPA references need specificity — adjusters need to know WHAT applies, not just that it exists
6. Labor ranges need wider real-world bands — current ranges are too tight for the variance seen in field
7. Valuation bands need updating — post-2022 supply chain pricing is significantly higher
8. Remount decision criteria needs its own module — it's too complex for a sub-topic
9. Every apparatus course needs "First 30 minutes on scene" practical guidance
10. MCSA-301 needs to come BEFORE MCSA-201 — you can't certify what you haven't audited
